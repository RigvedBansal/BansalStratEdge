FAQ

Real buyer questions.

Do you work with small companies?

Yes, if finance is already becoming a real operating function. We are usually a fit for funded startups and mid-sized businesses with enough complexity to benefit from better planning, capital discipline, and reporting. If you mainly need bookkeeping or basic admin support, we are probably not the right fit.

How is this different from consultants?

We keep the scope narrower and more practical. The goal is not a broad transformation deck. The goal is to diagnose what is slowing finance down, build the right system, and leave the team with something they can actually run.

Do you use AI or just advise?

Both, when it makes sense. We help identify the right finance use cases, shape the workflow, and implement lightweight solutions where the payoff is real. If AI adds noise, risk, or unnecessary complexity, we will say so.

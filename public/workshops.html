A. 2-hour executive workshop

AI in Finance: From Hype to Real Impact

Best for guest lectures, CXO forums, and compact executive cohorts
  1. 15 mins
    Setting the Context

    Evolution of finance | Why AI matters now | From hindsight to foresight

  2. 25 mins
    Where AI is Actually Being Used

    FP&A, risk, treasury, pricing | What's working vs. what's hype

  3. 25 mins
    AI in FP&A - The New Decision Engine

    From variance analysis to predictive insights | Data to model to insight to decision to value

  4. 20 mins
    The Big Risk: "Illusion of Precision"

    Why AI can mislead | Overfitting vs. real-world uncertainty | Who owns the decision when AI gets it wrong?

  5. 20 mins
    Implementation Playbook

    Where to start | Build vs. buy vs. partner | Governance and controls | Practical checklist

  6. 10-15 mins
    Interactive Case / Discussion

    "Should a bank use AI for capital allocation?" | Group discussion

  7. 5 mins
    Closing Insight

    "AI will not replace finance professionals. But finance professionals who use AI will replace those who don't."

Outcome

Participants gain clarity on real AI use cases, practical frameworks, and leadership implications.
